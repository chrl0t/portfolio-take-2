About

My name is Charlotte I'Anson and I am a full stack developer currently based in Leeds, UK.

Since graduating from university I have spent a lot of time travelling and experiencing the world. When the pandemic struck I lost my job and with all the free time I had I ended up taking a few online coding courses. This initial interest turned into something more, as I realised coding was something I could see myself doing as a career. Upon returning to the UK I took the plunge and enrolled at Northcoders, an intensive 14 week coding bootcamp.

I am currently continuing my learning and growth through building personal projects until my new job role begins in September.
