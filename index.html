About

My name is Charlotte I'Anson and I am a full stack developer currently based in Leeds, UK.

Having spent a lot of time since graduating travelling and experiencing the world, losing my job through the pandemic and then having an abundance of free time led me to coding. What started as an initial interest turned into something more, as I realised it was something I could see myself doing as a career. Upon returning to the UK I took the plunge and enrolled at Northcoders, an intensive 14 week coding bootcamp.

Here I learnt all the necessary skills to kick-start my career as a software developer and I am currently looking for my first job role.
